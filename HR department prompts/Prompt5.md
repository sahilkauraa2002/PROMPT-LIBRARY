# PROMPT 5: Interview Question Generation
**Prompt text**

We are in the HR department of an online skincare brand and are hiring a Social Media Head. Generate interview questions for shortlisted candidates focusing on their experience, skills, qualifications and certifications in social media management and digital marketing.

---

**Intended Workflow**

This prompt supports the interview preparation stage, where HR creates structured questions to assess candidates effectively. It ensures interviews are aligned with job requirements.

**Problem Being Solved**

Interviews often lack consistency, with questions varying across candidates. This can result in unfair evaluation and poor candidate assessment. The prompt ensures structured and relevant questions are generated.

**Automation Potential**

Automation potential is medium, as AI can generate relevant and structured questions. However, HR must review and customise them based on specific needs. It improves consistency but does not replace interviewer judgment.

**Output**

Certainly! Here is a set of interview questions tailored for candidates applying for the Social Media Head position at your online skincare brand, focusing on their experience, skills, qualifications, and certifications in social media management and digital marketing:

### Experience
1. Can you walk us through your experience in managing social media for a skincare or beauty brand? What were some of your key achievements?
2. How have you developed and executed social media strategies that align with overall business goals?
3. Describe a successful social media campaign you led. What was your role, and what metrics did you use to measure success?
4. How do you stay updated with the latest trends in social media and digital marketing, especially within the skincare or beauty industry?
5. Have you managed social media crises or negative feedback online? How did you handle the situation?

### Skills
6. Which social media platforms do you have the most experience with, and how do you tailor content for each platform?
7. How proficient are you with social media management tools (e.g., Hootsuite, Sprout Social, Buffer)? Can you give examples of how you’ve used these tools to improve efficiency?
8. What is your approach to content creation and curation? Do you have experience with graphic design or video editing tools?
9. How do you use analytics and data to inform your social media strategies? Can you share an example where data led you to change your approach?
10. What strategies do you use to increase engagement and follower growth organically?

### Qualifications and Certifications
11. What formal education or training do you have related to marketing, communications, or digital media?
12. Do you hold any certifications in social media marketing or digital marketing (e.g., Facebook Blueprint, Google Analytics, HubSpot, Hootsuite)?
13. How have these qualifications and certifications contributed to your effectiveness as a social media manager?
14. Are you familiar with any skincare industry-specific marketing regulations or compliance standards?

### Additional Insight
15. How would you balance creative storytelling with data-driven decision-making in your role?
16. What role do influencer marketing and partnerships play in your social media strategy?
17. How do you coordinate with other departments (e.g., product, customer service, sales) to ensure cohesive brand messaging?
18. What are the biggest challenges you foresee in managing social media for a skincare brand, and how would you address them?
19. How do you plan and manage social media budgets, including paid advertising campaigns?
20. Can you provide examples of how you have driven ROI through social media initiatives?

These questions should help you gauge the candidate’s practical experience, technical skills, strategic thinking, and industry knowledge relevant to the Social Media Head role at your skincare brand.

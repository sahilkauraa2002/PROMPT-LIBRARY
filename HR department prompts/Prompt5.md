# PROMPT 5: Interview Question Generation
**Prompt text**

We are in the HR department of an online skincare brand and are hiring a Social Media Head. Generate 15–20 well-structured interview questions for shortlisted candidates, organised into categories such as experience, skills, qualifications, and certifications related to social media management and digital marketing. Ensure the questions are specifically relevant to managing social media for a skincare or beauty brand and assess practical experience, technical expertise, industry knowledge, and any relevant certifications. And give them real life technical situations to test their knowledge. Include few questions regarding the candidate's future plans. The questions should be clear, concise, non-repetitive, and suitable for evaluating the candidate’s overall suitability for the role in a professional hiring process.

---

**Intended Workflow**

This prompt supports the interview preparation stage, where HR creates structured questions to assess candidates effectively. It ensures interviews are aligned with job requirements.

**Problem Being Solved**

Interviews often lack consistency, with questions varying across candidates. This can result in unfair evaluation and poor candidate assessment. The prompt ensures structured and relevant questions are generated.

**Automation Potential**

Automation potential is medium, as AI can generate relevant and structured questions. However, HR must review and customise them based on specific needs. It improves consistency but does not replace interviewer judgment.

**Output**

Certainly! Here’s a well-structured list of interview questions for shortlisted candidates applying for the Social Media Head position at your skincare brand, organized by relevant categories:

---

### Experience

1. **Can you describe your previous experience managing social media for skincare or beauty brands? What specific challenges did you face, and how did you overcome them?**

2. **Share a successful social media campaign you led for a skincare or beauty brand. What were the goals, strategies, and results?**

3. **How do you tailor social media content to resonate with skincare audiences versus general lifestyle audiences?**

4. **Describe a time when you had to handle negative feedback or a social media crisis related to skincare product efficacy or safety. How did you manage it?**

5. **What metrics do you prioritize when reporting the success of social media campaigns in the skincare industry?**

---

### Skills and Technical Expertise

6. **Which social media platforms have you found most effective for skincare brands, and why? How do you optimize content for each?**

7. **Explain your experience with social media advertising tools (e.g., Facebook Ads Manager, Instagram Promotions). How do you create targeted ad campaigns for skincare products?**

8. **How do you use data analytics and insights to adjust a skincare brand’s social media strategy? Can you provide an example where data led to a significant change?**

9. **Describe your process for content creation and curation specifically for skincare products that require product education and ingredient transparency.**

10. **Have you used influencer marketing to promote skincare products? How do you select influencers, and how do you measure the ROI of influencer partnerships?**

---

### Industry Knowledge and Certifications

11. **What are the current trends in digital marketing and social media within the skincare and beauty industry? How would you incorporate them into your strategy?**

12. **Are you familiar with regulations and compliance issues related to advertising skincare products on social media (e.g., claims about product benefits, FDA guidelines)? How do you ensure compliance?**

13. **Do you hold any certifications relevant to social media management or digital marketing (e.g., Facebook Blueprint, Google Ads, HubSpot Social Media Certification)? How have these certifications impacted your work?**

---

### Real-Life Technical Situations

14. **Situation: Engagement on Instagram for a new anti-aging serum is declining despite regular posts. How would you analyze and address this issue?**

15. **Situation: A competitor launches a viral skincare challenge on TikTok that draws significant attention away from your brand. How would you respond to regain visibility and engagement?**

16. **Situation: You notice that your Facebook ad spend is high but conversions on your skincare product page are low. Walk us through your troubleshooting steps.**

---

### Future Plans and Vision

17. **Where do you see the future of social media marketing for skincare brands heading in the next 3–5 years?**

18. **How do you plan to innovate or differentiate our skincare brand’s social media presence from competitors?**

19. **What are your professional development goals related to social media and digital marketing in the skincare industry?**

20. **If hired, what would be your 90-day plan to assess, improve, and grow our social media channels?**

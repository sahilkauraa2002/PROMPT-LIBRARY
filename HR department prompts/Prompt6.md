This prompt is used in the post-interview evaluation stage, where HR reviews notes and summarises candidate performance.

Interview notes are often unstructured and difficult to interpret, slowing decision-making. Important insights may be lost. This prompt organises feedback into clear summaries.

**Automation Potential**

Automation potential is medium to high, as AI can efficiently summarise information. Human oversight ensures accuracy and context.
